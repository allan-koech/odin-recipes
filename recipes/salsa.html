salsa

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Jalapeño pepper
  • Cilantro
  • Lime juice

Procedure

To prepare the salsa, start by finely chopping the tomatoes, red onion, and jalapeño pepper (removing the seeds for less heat, if preferred). Chop a handful of fresh cilantro and add it to the mix. Squeeze the juice of a lime over the ingredients and stir everything together in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and adjust the lime juice or jalapeño for desired tanginess and heat. Let the salsa sit for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld, then serve with chips, tacos, or any dish that could use a fresh, zesty kick!
